About

As the designer, the task involved converting Google Docs into visually compelling print and web reports for two significant projects. The responsibilities included adhering to established style guides for print materials, ensuring a cohesive and professional appearance. On the web, interactive elements were incorporated to enhance user engagement, and SEO optimization was implemented to improve online visibility. The design work also focused on meeting stringent accessibility standards, ensuring that both reports are inclusive and user-friendly. These reports addressed critical food policy issues, offering insights to inform decision-makers and promote community well-being.
