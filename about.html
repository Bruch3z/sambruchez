Sam Bruchez is a Swiss-American graphic designer based in NYC. Born and raised in the Val d'Entremont, Sam has combined his curiosity for his surroundings with his interest in graphic design.

He studied at the University of Vermont and at ECAL (Lausanne). Over the past ten years, Sam has gained rich experience working for sports and fashion brands in Zurich and New York. Some of his previous clients include The North Face, Head, and Nike.

In his personal design work, Sam is interested in exploring obscure archives in Valais, the Swiss canton where he grew up.
